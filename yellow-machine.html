137 Yellow Machines Officially Land at Freeport of Monrovia

Published: February 22, 2026 | Reported by: Peace Island Media

The vessel HJ SINGAPORE docked at the Freeport today with the first major consignment.

The Freeport of Monrovia was a scene of intense activity today as the vessel HJ SINGAPORE successfully docked, carrying a massive cargo of 137 heavy-duty "Yellow Machines."

This arrival represents the first official batch of the government's 285-unit procurement deal intended to fix the nation's deplorable road networks. Unlike the initial sample machines seen last year, this shipment includes a full range of technical equipment, from high-capacity excavators to specialized service trucks.

Arrival Details:

  • Vessel Name: HJ SINGAPORE
  • Total Machines: 137 Units
  • Supporting Gear: Multiple containers of spare parts and maintenance tools
  • Destination: National staging area before county distribution

During the offloading process, officials from the Yellow Machines Board of Authority (YMBOA) emphasized that these machines are part of a transparent $22 million deal. The equipment is scheduled to be distributed to all 15 counties, with each county expected to receive its own dedicated fleet for road maintenance.

For our community on Peace Island, this is a significant step. Improved road infrastructure means better access for transport, lower costs for goods, and a stronger connection between the 540 Community and the heart of Monrovia.
